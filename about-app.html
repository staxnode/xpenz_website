About Xpenz

Discover all the powerful tools and features designed to help you master your finances, hit your savings goals, and track every penny.

Core Capabilities

Xpenz provides complete visibility into your finances. From tracking a small coffee purchase to managing massive business expenses, everything is just a tap away.

  • Income & Expense Management: Categorize transactions, add notes and attachments, and track cash, bank, or card transactions.
  • Financial Dashboard: View current balance, total income, net cash flow, and financial summary cards at a glance.
  • Monthly & Yearly Reports: Compare spending over time, analyze cash flow trends, and easily export data in PDF, Excel, or CSV.

Advanced Tracking & Management

  • Multiple Accounts: Seamlessly manage cash wallets, bank accounts, credit cards, and digital wallets. Transfer money between accounts effortlessly.
  • Debt & Loan Tracker: Keep a record of money lent or borrowed, EMI payments, due dates, and payment history.
  • Bill & Subscription Management: Track recurring services like Netflix, Spotify, Internet, and Electricity, with proactive renewal reminders.
  • Recurring Transactions: Automate your salary, rent, utility bills, and loan payments to save time.
