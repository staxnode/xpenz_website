About XPENZ

Empowering everyone to take control of their financial lives through simplicity, automation, and intelligent insights.

Pocket Tracker is your sleek, secure, and intuitive financial companion built to give you full clarity over your money — without the complexity. Whether you're tracking day-to-day spending, managing savings, lending or borrowing, or keeping an eye on upcoming payments, Pocket Tracker keeps everything in one place so you can make smarter financial decisions with confidence.

Why Pocket Tracker Exists

In today's fast-paced world, financial stress can sneak up on anyone. Many expense apps are too cluttered, too transactional, or too detached from real financial life. Pocket Tracker was designed to strip away that noise, giving you an elegant, minimal, and powerful tool to manage your finances — not overwhelm them.

What Makes It Powerful

All-in-One Financial Hub

Track income, expenses, debts, lending, and payments in one unified space, so nothing slips through the cracks

🔔 Smart Reminders for Dues

Never forget a payment or repayment — set due reminders and stay on top of your obligations.

📊 Insights That Matter

Weekly and monthly summaries help you understand your spending patterns, spot trends, and make adjustments.

🎯 Budget Your Way

Set budgets for different categories, receive alerts when you overspend, and stay disciplined.

🔐 Secure & Private

Your financial data stays in your control. With optional cloud sync and strong privacy design, your data is safe, always.

📄 Export & Share

Generate clean financial reports in CSV or PDF — perfect for personal reflection or sharing with someone you trust.

📈 Advanced Reporting

Access visual reports — including due summaries, monthly breakdowns, and category-wise analytics for deeper financial understanding

Our Mission

We believe financial stability shouldn't be a luxury — it should be accessible, straightforward, and deeply personal. Pocket Tracker is committed to empowering individuals, freelancers, small-business owners, and families to build better financial habits, make informed decisions, and shape a more confident financial future.
