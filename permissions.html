App Permissions

Transparency is key. Here is a detailed breakdown of the permissions Xpenz requires and exactly how we use them.

To provide a secure and seamless experience, the app requests certain permissions. These permissions enable core features, such as:

  • Managing and connecting with customers and suppliers
  • Tracking sales and transactions
  • Uploading and accessing business-related documents, images, and media
  • Sending important notifications and alerts
  • Ensuring secure login and authentication

The following section lists all permissions requested by the app along with their purpose, so you can understand why each permission is needed and how it helps you manage your business effectively.

Requested Permissions

  • INTERNET – Access the internet to fetch and sync app data.
  • READ_CONTACTS – Read your contacts to help you connect with friends or share content.
  • WRITE_CONTACTS – Save new contacts created through the app.
  • READ_PHONE_STATE – Access device information for security, authentication, and call management.
  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE / WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE – Read and save files on your device (photos, videos, documents).
  • READ_MEDIA_IMAGES / READ_MEDIA_VIDEO / READ_MEDIA_AUDIO – Access media files for sharing, viewing, or editing within the app.
  • ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION – Get approximate location to provide location-based features.
  • ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION – Get precise location for accurate maps, navigation, and location-based recommendations.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS – Send notifications for alerts, reminders, and updates.
  • USE_BIOMETRIC – Enable login and authentication using fingerprint or face recognition.
  • CAMERA – Capture photos or videos directly from the app.

Notes & Instructions for Users:

Managing Permissions:

You can view, manage, or revoke any permission at any time through your device settings:

  • On Android 13+: Go to Settings → Apps → Xpenz → Permissions.
  • On older Android versions: Go to Settings → Apps → Xpenz → App Permissions.

Why Permissions Matter:

Each permission enables a specific app feature. For example:

  • Camera access allows you to upload profile photos or documents.
  • Location access helps you find nearby services or deliveries.

Data Safety:

Xpenz Platform only uses your data to provide app functionality. We do not share your personal data with third parties.

Notifications:

You can turn notifications on or off in your device settings. This allows you to control alerts for promotions, updates, and reminders.

Following these instructions ensures that Xpenz Platform works optimally and your data remains safe.
